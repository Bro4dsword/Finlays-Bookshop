How it works

  1. Choose membership, borrowing, a custom book, or a question.
  2. Send a short request to Finlay.
  3. Finlay confirms membership, availability, delivery/collection, and payment if needed.

For now this is a simple request form, not a checkout. Sensible grown-up infrastructure, sadly less exciting than a dragon cashier.
