Parent-reviewed requests

Join, donate, or trade

Use this to ask about membership, donations, trades, or borrowing a book. No payment is taken on this page — Finlay checks the request first and replies with the next step.

How it works

  1. Choose Bronze, Silver, or Gold membership.
  2. Say whether you want to donate, trade, borrow, or ask a question.
  3. Send a short request to Finlay.
  4. Finlay confirms availability, delivery/collection, and the next step.

For now this is a simple request form, not a checkout. Sensible grown-up infrastructure, sadly less exciting than a dragon cashier.
